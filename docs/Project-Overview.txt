Project Overview                                                                   
                                                                                     
  This is a Google Ads campaign system for Cal Preserving, a Bay Area exterior wood  
  preservation and restoration company (decks, siding, fences, pressure washing).    
                                                                                     
  The Problem                                                                        
                                                                                     
  Cal Preserving relied on organic search and referrals, creating unpredictable lead 
  flow that's hard to scale.                                                         
                                                                                     
  The Solution                                                                       
                                                                                     
  A structured, phased paid search system that:                                      
  - Targets high-value customers for premium wood restoration services               
  - Filters out low-value traffic (DIY, budget seekers, composite materials)         
  - Enables geographic and market expansion                                          
  - Improves lead quality through precise targeting                                  
                                                                                     
  Three Phases                                                                       
  ┌───────┬──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐               
  │ Phase │                          Scope                           │               
  ├───────┼──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤               
  │ 1     │ Bay Area + Residential only (baseline launch)            │               
  ├───────┼──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤               
  │ 2     │ Adds Fringe geographies (extended service regions)       │               
  ├───────┼──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤               
  │ 3     │ Adds Commercial/HOA personas with separate landing pages │               
  └───────┴──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘               

  Key Components                                                                     
                                                                                     
  - Campaign Masters - CSV files mapping keywords → ad groups → landing pages        
  - Landing Page Specs - 5 residential + 5 commercial page designs                   
  - Segmentation Taxonomy - Definitions for geo, persona, surface, intent            
  - SFDC Mapping - Web form → Salesforce field alignment                             
  - Reporting Spec - KPIs (CPL, ROAS, CAC) and dashboard structure                   
  - QA Checklist - Launch validation process

  Target Personas                                                                    
                                                                                     
  Residential                                                                        
  - Homeowners, renters with authority, property sellers/buyers                      
  - Motivated by: gray/aged wood, peeling, mold, upcoming events, property sales     
  - Lower surface knowledge ("Is this cedar?")                                       
  - Uses "near me" searches, city names                                              
  - CTA: "Request a Free Estimate"                                                   
  - Key message: "You don't pay until you're happy"                                  
                                                                                     
  Commercial/HOA                                                                     
  - Property managers, facility managers, HOA boards, multi-unit owners              
  - Motivated by: maintenance cycles, liability, board requirements, reserve budgets 
  - Higher technical knowledge                                                       
  - Searches by region, uses "proposal" language                                     
  - CTA: "Request a Proposal"                                                        
  - Key message: Risk mitigation, compliance, reliability                            
                                                                                     
  Keyword Strategy                                                                   
                                                                                     
  47 keywords across 5 surfaces using Exact + Phrase match only (no Broad)

  ├───────────────────┼────────────────────────────────────────────┤

  ├───────────────────┼────────────────────────────────────────────┤

  ├───────────────────┼────────────────────────────────────────────┤

  ├───────────────────┼────────────────────────────────────────────┤

  ├───────────────────┼────────────────────────────────────────────┤

  Negative Keywords block:                                                           
  - DIY/tutorials, job boards                                                        
  - Home Depot, Lowe's, Amazon                                                       
  - Composite brands (Trex, TimberTech, Azek)                                        
  - Materials: PVC, vinyl, composite, fiber cement
  Landing Pages                                                                      
                                                                                     
  8-section standard layout:                                                         
  1. Hero with CTA                                                                   
  2. Pain/Problem                                                                    
  3. Services                                                                        
  4. Process (6-8 steps)                                                             
  5. Why Cal Preserving                                                              
  6. Service Areas                                                                   
  7. FAQs                                                                            
  8. Footer CTA                                                                      
                                                                                     
  10 total LPs in Phase 3: 5 surfaces × 2 personas

  Notable Business Rules                                                             
                                                                                     
  - Bay Area vs Fringe: Different job minimums (Fringe higher due to travel)         
  - Form requires: Property type, at least 1 surface, full address, contact info,    
  timing                                                                             
  - GCLID capture for offline conversion tracking                                    
  - No Broad match to avoid composite/DIY traffic leakage                            
  - Phase-gated rollout: Each phase builds on the last without rework

  Implementation Specifics                                                           
                                                                                     
  Campaign Structure by Phase                                                        
                                                                                     
  Phase 1 (Bay Area, Mixed Persona): 5 campaigns                                     
  - Bay Area Deck, Siding, Structures, Pressure, Catch-All                           
                                                                                     
  Phase 2 (Adds Fringe): 8+ campaigns                                                
  - Duplicates Phase 1 for Fringe geography                                          
  - Fringe omits Pressure & Furniture (volume economics)                             
                                                                                     
  Phase 3 (Persona Split): Full matrix                                               
  - Every campaign splits into Residential + Commercial versions                     
  - Example: Bay Area Deck - Residential, Bay Area Deck - Commercial
